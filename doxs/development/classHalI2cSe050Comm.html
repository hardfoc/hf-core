Detailed Description

CRTP adapter mapping BaseI2c primitives to se050::I2cTransceiveInterface.

Reset GPIO
When reset_gpio is non-null, HardwareReset() asserts reset with **SetInactive()**, waits 2 ms, then releases with **SetActive()** and waits 10 ms (same cadence as the ESP-IDF reference transport). Configure **BaseGpio** polarity so this sequence matches your SE_RESET wiring (typical nRESET: assert = drive low = logical inactive on an active-high GPIO).
